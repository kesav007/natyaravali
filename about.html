Manjusree has been trained under the able guidance of her Guru Shri B.Sudheer Rao who resides in Warangal. Having had her Diploma in Kuchipudi dance style and a Certificate in Bharatnatyam, she not only has immense interest in the art form but also excelled in the subject by escorting her Guru in many dance events in India. She continued her thirst for dance by getting trained under Late Sri.Vempati Chinna Satyam garu and Sri.Vempati Ravi Shankar garu from Chennai.

She gave performances in prestigious festivals like Ananya Dance Festival, Khajuraho festival and Chidambaram dance festivals.She not only bagged Rolling shield in All India Competitions in Music and Dance but also is a Central Scholarship holder from CCRT in the field of Kuchipudi dance. She was talented enough to perform Pancharatna Keertana at the age of 10 and bagged a Natya Mayuri award at that very young age. She has been awarded Nritya Kinnera award from Telangana Govt. She is also a graded artist in Doordarshan Hyderabad.

She escorted her Guru in almost all of his endeavors and excelled in Nattuvangam and teaching from her childhood. She used to take classes when her Guru has perceived his BA and MA in Dance from Hyderabad. Identifying her talent and interest in dance Guru Shri Sudheer Rao encouraged her to enact new roles in every dance ballet. She performed as Narada and Rukmini in Sri Krishna Parijatham dance ballet, as Hiranyakasipa in Prahlada Yakshaganam and Hiranyaksha in Mahishasura Mardhini, as Shiva in Shiva Puranam dance ballets. She involved herself to her best in which ever role she performed.

Having achieved a lot in her childhood she would like to continue teaching to younger generation kids and encourage them to perform on several stages.
